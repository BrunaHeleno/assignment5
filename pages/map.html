Map

City

  • When you are in the city you can do a few things:
  • Buy items, the items you are allowed to but depends on the prosperity of your team; You can carry 1 item for your head, body, feet; the numbers of potions depends on the level of your character; and for your hands depends on if the item is single-handed (2 items) or double-handed (1 item)
  • Level up your character: after a long journey your character acquires experience, with more experience, more abilities
  • Donate to the Sanctuary of the Great Oak, when you donate you receive two bless, which can help a lot to kill the monsters
  • Do the City Event, city event is a card with a situation and two options, each option has a consequence, good or bad
  • Tip: choose the items carefully, some are better for specific characters
  • Tip 2: when doing the city event, try to think as your character, for example, my character is very curious, so it is not because I wouldn't go inside an abondened build to check a strange noise, that means my character wouldn't go either, because I'm sure he would

Route

  • On route the only thing you can do is Route Event
  • It works the same way as the City Event, there is a situation, with two options, each one with a consequence
  • Tip: playing with my friend, we decide that just one person reads and explains the situation and options to the others, two reasons for that:
  • First: it's faster than everybody reading
  • Second: some of the cards are returned to the deck, so the consequences of the option we didn't choose remains a secret

Scenario

  • It is time to deal with the monsters!
  • The scenario may have requirements to be played
  • Every scenario has a specific objective
  • Tip: pay attention to the requirements, just because the scenario is present on the map does not mean you can play it
  • Tip: just one person builds the scenario, that way the others in the team don't see all the monsters and can experience fairer decisions
  • There are obstacles, difficult terrains and traps (images below)
