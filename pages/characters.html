Characters

To Start

  • There are six characters to choose
  • You are allowed to see their icons (images below) and their names
  • Every one belongs to a different class with a unique set of skills
  • If you do not like the character you chose, you are allowed to change to another, but just once
  • Tip: do not think too much, choose the icon you like the most at first

Mission

  • As soon you chose your character, you are going to receive two mission cards
  • Read carefuly and choose one
  • This mission is the reason for your character to be a mercenary, go to work
  • Tip: try to think as your character

Retire

  • The mission is completed and it is time to retire
  • This step is necessary to follow the instructions on the mission card, you will be allowed to open one box (a new character - images below) or an envelope (envelope X)
  • Following the rules, when your character retires, all items and money are returned to the city
  • Tip: me and my friend changed this rule, when our characters retire they give the items to the new ones as legacy and a "welcome" gift
