Monsters

  • There are two types of monsters: Elite and Normal
  • Elite always goes first and normally, they are stronger than the normal ones
  • The monsters always attack the nearest character
  • Tip: when executing the monster's action, try to think as the monster, I know it's nice to win, but it needs to be fair

Action

  • Each player chooses two cards and plays them faced down
  • When every one is ready, we turn up the cards and say the number of our initiative (number in the middle of the card)
  • After all players have said initiative, we turn over the monsters card, the one with the lowest initiative goes first
  • In your turn you choose the actions in your card, being one action at the top of one card and one at the bottom of the other card
  • Tip: you can choose any one of the two numbers on the cards as initiative, doesn't need be the lowest, sometimes it's better to go later
