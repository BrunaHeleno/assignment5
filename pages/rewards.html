Rewards

Treasure

  • Some scenarios have treasures
  • When you open the treasure it can be something good or bad
  • Tip: as soon you get the treasure, check what's inside, sometimes the "reward" is to be used during the scenario

New Location

  • Some scenarios, when completed, give you new locations to go to
  • When the scenarios are connected, it is not necessary go to the city, do the route event and then go to the new location, you can choose to go directly
  • Tip: me and my friend always go back to the city, so we can do the city and route event (and maybe get something good for the next scenario), level up our character and discuss where we're going next

Achievement

  • There are two types of achievement: Global and Party
  • Global achievements affect all the different party that are playing
  • Party achievements affect just your party, or in other words, just your team
  • The achievements work like a key to open and lock some scenarios
