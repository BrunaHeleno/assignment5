Welcome to Gloomhaven

Gloomhaven is a cooperative game of card-driven combat set in a persistent fantasy campaing which will change and grow as you play it.

The game is full of secrets, such as locked envelopes and boxes, to not spoil the mistery for those who are interested in play, in this website we are going to show only the things that are allowed to be seen when you start play. The focus will be teach the rules with some tips

It's a grim but lucrative business being a mercenary out on the edge of civilization. You must explore the harsh wilderness and engage in deep tactical combat, where the decisions you make have an impact not only on your own character, but also the world around you

Are you ready to become the best mercenary in the world?
